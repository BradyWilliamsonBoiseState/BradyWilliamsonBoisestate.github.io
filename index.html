Rocks Against Darkness

Rocks Against Darkness is an ongoing Unity virtual reality project. This project is my pride and joy. I've built this from from the ground up, learning about how to program and design for VR as well as how to manage a larger scale project on my own. Its grown from an abstracted, moving diorama to a small world with simple interactions. Its not finished yet but I can't wait to show off the final product.

Welcome to Greentide

In welcome to Greentide you manage an augmented reality city spawned off a marker. Playing the game means keeping your resources in balance by choosing between different presented props. I was responsible for creating our resource management system, and ensuring that it would interact seamlessly with our event system and the prompts it provided. This system also handled the win and lose conditions as well as prompting the visible changes in game state.
